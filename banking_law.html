Banking Regulations and Compliance

Banks and financial institutions must comply with a myriad of regulations, both national and international. Our legal experts provide guidance on adhering to:

Basel III: International standards on banking capital requirements, stress testing, and market liquidity risk.

Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML): Regulatory frameworks to prevent money laundering and ensure the integrity of financial transactions.

Consumer Protection Laws: Safeguarding the rights of customers, including fair lending practices and disclosure requirements.

Banking Transactions and Agreements

From opening accounts to securing loans, banking transactions require legal oversight. We assist with:

Drafting and Reviewing Banking Agreements: Loan contracts, credit agreements, and more.

Dispute Resolution: Addressing issues related to banking transactions, fraud, or breach of contract.

Credit Facilities: Including mortgages, business loans, and personal loans.

Corporate Banking and Financing

For businesses, banking law extends beyond simple transactions to complex financial agreements. Our services include:

Corporate Lending and Financing Structures: Securing loans, negotiating interest rates, and managing liabilities.

Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A): Legal advice on financing strategies and compliance in M&A transactions.

Securities: Assisting with the issuance and regulation of corporate bonds, stocks, and other financial securities.

Banking Litigation and Dispute Resolution

Disputes in banking can arise from breach of contract, fraudulent activity, or financial mismanagement. Our firm provides:

Litigation Services: Representing clients in court for banking disputes, including breach of loan agreements, misrepresentation, and fraud.

Arbitration and Mediation: Alternative dispute resolution methods to resolve banking issues outside the courtroom, ensuring quicker, more cost-effective outcomes.

Debt Recovery and Restructuring

Banks often face challenges related to non-performing loans and bad debts. We help with:

Debt Recovery: Legal processes for recovering overdue loans from individuals or businesses.

Restructuring and Insolvency: Advising on debt restructuring, liquidation, or bankruptcy proceedings for businesses facing financial difficulties.
